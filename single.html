ABOUT THE EVENT

We invite you to a big concert by Artem Pivovarov and his orchestra at the Palace of Sports in Kyiv!

Last year, Artem Pivovarov presented a high-tech show at the capital's Palace of Sports, gathering three sellouts in a row. This time, the artist will play a large concert with an orchestra in the same hall, which will be the only opportunity to attend Pivovarov's full-length performance in Ukraine in 2025.

On November 7, a unique show awaits you, which will amaze with its content and scale, high-quality music and unique atmosphere. Each viewer will be able to feel the irrepressible energy of the Pivovarov Team, be charged with positive emotions and enjoy their favorite songs accompanied by the orchestra, in particular from the new album "Your Poems, My Notes PT.2". At the concert you will hear live "Drum", "Deja Vu", "Mirage", "Thoughts", "Manifest", "There in the Poplar" and other hits.

Artem Pyvovarov has traveled the world, popularizing Ukrainian culture in Europe, the USA and Canada. Over the past three years, the artist has played over 300 charity concerts in Ukraine and abroad, gathering full halls everywhere. Each of the performances was charitable and this concert at the Palace of Sports is no exception. Pyvovarov's team will direct the funds raised to fulfill the requests of brigades and units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, which will help our defenders in the fight for freedom, independence and a better future for Ukraine.

Don't miss the opportunity to feel the crazy Ukrainian drive and join a good cause!


General radio partner: Hit FM

Show created by: Pivovarov Production

Organizer: Magicbox Association
